## Marketing Tools

Attract new clients and keep existing ones engaged with powerful marketing features.

### Email Campaigns

- **Drag-and-drop editor** — create beautiful emails without design skills
- **Templates library** — 50+ pre-built templates for fitness & wellness
- **Segmentation** — target clients by membership, attendance, or purchase history
- **A/B testing** — optimize subject lines and content
- **Analytics** — track opens, clicks, and conversions

### Referral Program

Turn happy clients into your best marketers:

1. Client refers a friend via unique link
2. Friend signs up and makes first purchase
3. Both receive a reward (customizable: discount, free class, credit)
