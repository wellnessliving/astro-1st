## Online Scheduling

Let your clients book anytime, anywhere with our intelligent scheduling system.

### Key Features

- **24/7 online booking** — clients book from your website or app
- **Staff calendar management** — individual and shared calendars
- **Recurring appointments** — set up weekly, biweekly, or monthly sessions
- **Buffer times** — automatic breaks between appointments
- **Capacity management** — set class sizes and waitlists
- **Timezone support** — perfect for virtual sessions

### How It Works

1. Set your availability and services
2. Share your booking link or embed on your website
3. Clients pick a time and book instantly
4. Both parties receive confirmation and reminders
