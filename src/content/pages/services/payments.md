## Payment Processing

Accept payments effortlessly with built-in processing — no third-party integrations needed.

### Accepted Methods

- Credit & debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- ACH / bank transfers
- Apple Pay & Google Pay
- Gift cards and store credits

### Features

- **Auto-billing** — charge memberships and packages automatically
- **Split payments** — let clients pay with multiple methods
- **Refunds & credits** — process in one click
- **PCI compliant** — bank-level security standards
- **Transparent fees** — 2.49% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Next-day deposits** — funds in your account fast

### Revenue Recovery

Automatically retry failed payments and notify clients:

| Attempt | Timing | Method |
|---------|--------|--------|
| 1st retry | Day 3 | Auto-charge |
| 2nd retry | Day 7 | Auto-charge + email |
| 3rd retry | Day 14 | Email + SMS |
| Final notice | Day 21 | Account pause notification |
