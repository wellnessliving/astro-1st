## Getting Started

### How long does it take to set up?

Most businesses are up and running within a week. Our onboarding team guides you through every step — importing client data, configuring schedules, and setting up payments.

### Do I need technical knowledge?

Not at all. Our platform is designed for business owners, not developers. If you can use email, you can use WellnessLiving.

### Can I import data from my current system?

Yes. We support imports from MindBody, Zen Planner, ClubReady, and most other platforms. Our team handles the migration for you.

## Pricing

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

### What happens after the trial?

You choose the plan that fits your business. Plans start at $49/month for a single location.

### Are there any long-term contracts?

No. All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

## Features

### Does the mobile app work offline?

The app requires an internet connection for booking and payments, but clients can view their upcoming schedule and saved content offline.

### Can I customize the look and feel?

Absolutely. Your client-facing app and booking pages are fully branded with your logo, colors, and domain.

### Do you integrate with other tools?

Yes. We integrate with Google Calendar, Stripe, Mailchimp, Zoom, and many more via our API and Zapier.
